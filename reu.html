CAT Vehicle Research Experience for Undergraduates

ECE 492 - CAT Vehicle Research Experience for Undergraduates

A 10 Week NSF funded program to prepare undergraduate students to kickstart research in autonomous driving.
Apply at http://catvehicle.arizona.edu

Summer 2019

Course discussion page: https://piazza.com/class/jwck37wbf052ai

Course outline

  1. Week 01-02 : Short courses preparing students for projects
  2. Week 03-05: Project design and literature review
  3. Week 06-08: Project implementation
  4. Week 09: Testing phase
  5. Week 10: Demo week
