Research

My primary research work focuses on developing new methods for understanding intelligent transportation and impact of introducing autonomous vehicles in human traffic. I am currently developing new simulation tools for autonomous driving to simulate large scale traffic scenario with high fidelity physics based models. I am one of the lead developer for CAT Vehicle Testbed working with an ongoing effort to bring this simulation tool to public. Over the last few years, community has greatly benefited from the CAT Vehicle Testbed. Please check them out at CPS-VO's testbed group:
https://cps-vo.org/group/CATVehicleTestbed.

In addition to that, I am learning Adaptive Optics with an aim to apply Machine Learning models to achieve better results that is currently not possible with traditional Adaptive Optics systems. If you like to collaborate on this topic, please connect with me.
