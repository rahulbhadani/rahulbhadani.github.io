About Me

Rahul Bhadani is a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona. He is associated with Compositional Systems Lab at U of A. His primary research is related to transportation Cyber-physical Systems (CPS) and modeling and simulation for autonomous-driving. He is currently working under the supervision of Dr. Jonathan Sprinkle. He has project management and leadership experience gained while serving as a mentor for REU undergraduate interns every Summer since 2016, supervising them on projects related with controller design for autonomous driving and detection/perception algorithms using a range of sensors including LIDAR, Stereocamera and GPS.
