Rahul Bhadani

Currently, a Post-doctoral Researcher at Vanderbilt University PhD, Electrical & Computer Engineering, The University of Arizona
Connected and Autonomous Vehicles | Mobility | Quantum Information Theory

LinkedIn Github UofA Page YouTube Medium

I obtained my PhD in May 2022 from the University of Arizona in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I study the driving behavior of vehicles in human-autonomous vehicles mixed traffic through simulation and field experiments; quantum information theory; and statistical modeling of physical and biological systems. My research is built on the foundation of cyber-physical systems, software engineering principles, and control engineering.

Previously, I have taught Software Engineering (Spring 2021), and have mentored and co-taught Programming-II C++, ROS, and CAT Vehicle Research Experience for Undergraduates (CAT Vehicle REU, Summer 2016 - Summer 2020).
