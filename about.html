About Me

I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona where I work in Compositional Systems Lab at U of A. My primary research is related to transportation Cyber-physical Systems (CPS) and modeling and simulation for autonomous-driving under the supervision of Dr. Jonathan Sprinkle. I have project management and leadership experience gained while serving as a mentor for REU undergraduate interns every summer since 2016, supervising them on projects related with controller design for autonomous driving and detection/perception algorithms using a range of sensors including LIDAR, Stereocamera and GPS.

Research Interest: Intelligent Transportation, Cyber-physical Systems, Machine Learning, Quantum Information Science
